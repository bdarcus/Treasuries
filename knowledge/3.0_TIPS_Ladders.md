## TIPS Ladder Terminology

**DARA (Desired Annual Real Amount):** Target total cash flow for a funded year in real (inflation-adjusted) terms
- Replaces DAA from 2.0 Bond Ladders
- "Real" means in today's dollars

**ARA (Annual Real Amount):** Actual cash flow produced by the ladder for a funded year in real terms
- Replaces AA from 2.0 Bond Ladders
- May differ from DARA due to rounding

**Note on "Real":** All principal and interest values are inflation-adjusted, so "real" is implied throughout

---

## Gap Years

**Gap Years:** Years within ladder period where no TIPS exist (currently 2037, 2038, 2039)

**Synthetic TIPS for Gap Years:**
- Created to maintain ladder continuity
- Maturity: February 15 of gap year
- Index ratio: 1.0 (assumes refCPI = dated refCPI)
- Price: 100 (par)

---

## Synthetic TIPS Construction

**Yield Interpolation:**
```
Anchors: Latest maturity before gaps, first maturity after gaps
Example: Jan 2036 and Feb 2040

syntheticYield = lowerYield + (gapDate - lowerDate) * (upperYield - lowerYield) / (upperDate - lowerDate)
```

**Coupon Calculation:**
```
syntheticCoupon = MAX(0.00125, FLOOR(syntheticYield * 100 / 0.125) * 0.00125)
```

## Algorithm

Use 2.0 Bond Ladders algorithm with these modifications:

1. **Add:** Identify gap years, create synthetic TIPS
2. **Substitute:** Use all inflation-adjusted formulas from 2.1 TIPS Basics:
   - Inflation-Adjusted Principal instead of nominal principal
   - Inflation-Adjusted Annual Interest instead of nominal annual interest
   - TIPS-specific Last-Year Interest rules (month < 7 → 0.5, month ≥ 7 → 1.0)
   - P+I per Bond (TIPS) formula
